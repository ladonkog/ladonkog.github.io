Experimental Aerodynamics: Supersonic Wing at Low Mach Number

Project Overview

This project involved the design, manufacturing, and wind tunnel testing of a supersonic wing at low Mach numbers. Our team aimed to improve the aerodynamic stability and performance of the wing through rigorous testing and analysis.

Key Contributions

  • Led an 8-member team in designing and manufacturing the supersonic wing using aluminum spars and ribs.
  • Conducted structural load analysis using Siemens Femap, verifying hand calculations.
  • Achieved 10% better aerodynamic stability than initial predictions.
