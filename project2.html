Aircraft Preliminary Design: Hybrid-Electric Military Logistics Aircraft

Project Overview

This project focused on the design and analysis of a hybrid-electric military logistics aircraft, targeting efficiency and performance improvements. The design process involved optimization of the propulsion system, tail geometry, and overall stability.

Key Contributions

  • Performed initial tail sizing and geometry, optimizing aerodynamic efficiency and stability.
  • Selected hybrid-electric propulsion components and optimized battery packs to meet aircraft energy requirements.
  • Conducted stick-fixed stability analysis to evaluate aircraft control and performance.
